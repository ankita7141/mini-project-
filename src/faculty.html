Emergency Protocols

Important procedures to follow in case of medical emergencies on campus.

Medical Emergency

Step 1: Call Campus Security at (555) 911-9111

Step 2: Provide your exact location and nature of emergency

Step 3: Stay with the person if safe to do so

Step 4: Follow instructions from emergency responders

Mental Health Crisis

Step 1: Contact Campus Counseling at (555) 234-5678

Step 2: For immediate danger, call 911

Step 3: Stay with the person until help arrives

Step 4: Notify department chair or administrator

Building Evacuation

Step 1: Follow evacuation routes to designated safe areas

Step 2: Assist students and colleagues as needed

Step 3: Account for all individuals in your care

Step 4: Report to assembly point and check in

Health Policy Awareness

Important health policies and procedures that faculty members should be aware of.

Sick Leave Policy

Faculty members are entitled to 15 paid sick days per academic year. Unused sick days may be carried forward up to a maximum of 45 days. Documentation may be required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days.

View Full Policy

Family Medical Leave

Eligible faculty may take up to 12 weeks of unpaid, job-protected leave for specified family and medical reasons. Continuation of health benefits is provided during this period.

View Full Policy

Workplace Accommodations

The institution provides reasonable accommodations for faculty with disabilities or health conditions. Requests should be submitted to the Faculty Affairs Office with appropriate documentation.

View Full Policy

Health Insurance Benefits

Comprehensive health insurance plans are available to full-time faculty members. Open enrollment occurs each November for the following calendar year.
