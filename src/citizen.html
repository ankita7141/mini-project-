Basic First Aid Information

Essential first aid steps for common emergencies.

Burns

Cool the burn with running water for 10-15 minutes. Cover with sterile dressing.

Choking

Perform abdominal thrusts (Heimlich maneuver) if conscious. Call for emergency help.

Snakebite

Keep the person calm and immobilized. Do not cut the wound or try to suck out venom.

Road Accident

Check for danger first. Do not move the injured unless necessary. Call emergency services.

For Elderly & Children

Special healthcare tips for vulnerable age groups.

Hydration Tips

Elderly should drink at least 8 glasses of water daily. Children need frequent hydration during play.

Medication Reminders

Use pill organizers and set alarms. Never skip prescribed medications.

Vaccination Schedule

Follow the national immunization schedule for children. Elderly should get flu and pneumonia vaccines.
