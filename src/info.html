Frequently Asked Questions

How do I prepare for my surgery? +

Your surgeon will provide specific instructions, but general guidelines include:

  • Do not eat or drink after midnight before your surgery (unless instructed otherwise)
  • Arrange for someone to drive you home after the procedure
  • Wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing
  • Leave valuables at home
  • Bring your insurance card and photo ID
What insurance plans do you accept? +

We accept most major insurance plans, including:

  • Blue Cross Blue Shield
  • Aetna
  • Cigna
  • UnitedHealthcare
  • Medicare
  • Medicaid

Please contact our billing department at (555) 123-BILL to verify your specific coverage.

How can I access my test results? +

Test results are typically available through our patient portal within 3-5 business days. For urgent results, your provider will contact you directly. If you need assistance accessing the portal, please call our medical records department at (555) 123-RECS.

What COVID-19 safety measures are in place? +

We continue to follow CDC guidelines to ensure patient and staff safety:

  • Enhanced cleaning protocols throughout the facility
  • Mask requirements in clinical areas
  • Social distancing in waiting areas
  • Visitor restrictions when necessary
  • Virtual visit options for appropriate appointments
How do I transfer medical records to MediAssist? +

To transfer your medical records to us, you'll need to:

  1. Complete an Authorization for Release of Health Information form
  2. Provide the complete name and address of your previous provider
  3. Submit the form to our Health Information Management department

Forms are available at our reception desk or can be downloaded from our website.
