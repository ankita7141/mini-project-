Our Journey

From a small community clinic to a leading healthcare institution

1985

Foundation

MediAssist Hospital was founded by Dr. Robert Johnson with a vision to provide comprehensive healthcare to the growing community.

1995

Expansion

Added a new wing with advanced surgical suites and a dedicated maternity ward, doubling our capacity to serve patients.

2005

Technology Advancement

Became one of the first hospitals in the region to implement electronic health records and telemedicine capabilities.

2015

Recognition

Received the National Quality Healthcare Award for excellence in patient care and safety standards.

2020

Pandemic Response

Established a specialized COVID-19 response unit and implemented innovative safety protocols to protect patients and staff.

2023

Future Vision

Launching our new Center for Medical Innovation focused on research and cutting-edge treatments.
