Health Awareness

Eye Strain Prevention

Tips to reduce digital eye strain:

  • Follow the 20-20-20 rule (every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds)
  • Adjust screen brightness to match your surroundings
  • Position screen at arm's length and slightly below eye level
  • Use artificial tears if eyes feel dry
  • Blink frequently

Posture Correction

Maintain proper posture while studying:

  • Sit with back straight and shoulders back
  • Keep feet flat on the floor
  • Position screen at eye level
  • Take regular breaks to stretch
  • Use a chair with good lumbar support

Balanced Diet for Cadets

Protein-Rich Foods

  • Eggs and lean meats
  • Fish and seafood
  • Dairy products (milk, yogurt, paneer)
  • Legumes (beans, lentils, chickpeas)
  • Nuts and seeds
  • Soy products (tofu, tempeh)

Energy Foods for Training

  • Whole grains (oats, brown rice)
  • Sweet potatoes
  • Bananas and other fruits
  • Energy bars (homemade preferred)
  • Dried fruits and nuts mix

Hydration Tips

  • Drink water throughout the day
  • Consume electrolyte drinks during intense training
  • Monitor urine color (light yellow is ideal)
  • Avoid excessive caffeine
  • Include water-rich foods (cucumber, watermelon)

Posture Awareness Guide

Maintain proper posture for parade and daily activities.

Standing Posture (Attention)

  • Heels together, feet at 45° angle
  • Knees straight but not locked
  • Shoulders back and down
  • Chest lifted, chin parallel to ground
  • Arms straight, thumbs along trouser seams
  • Weight evenly distributed on both feet

Marching Posture

  • Maintain upright posture while moving
  • Swing arms 6 inches to front and 3 inches to rear
  • Step length of 30 inches
  • Lift feet about 6 inches off the ground
  • Keep head and eyes forward

Hygiene for Camps

Special care for hygiene during field conditions and camps.

Camp Hygiene Checklist

  • Always use purified/boiled water for drinking
  • Wash hands with soap before handling food
  • Keep eating utensils clean and covered
  • Dispose of waste properly, away from living areas
  • Use insect repellent to prevent mosquito bites
  • Keep nails trimmed and clean
  • Change into dry clothes if wet from rain or sweat
  • Use designated toilet areas only
  • Report any health issues immediately to camp leader
